OUR ROOMS

Single

Spacious, bright and outward facing rooms measuring 27 m2 and totally refurbished. The room comes with double bed or twin beds with Dreamax mattress (manufactured and designed exclusively by Flex for Meliá Hotels International), a modern, fully equipped bathroom finished in top quality bronze coloured ceramics and an independent hallway-dressing area. It also has a home automation system which automatically regulates the temperature of the room based on guest presence or absence from the room. All the rooms have a magnificent hallway-dressing area, as well as a room off the bathroom, which is independent of the room as it has double doors.
