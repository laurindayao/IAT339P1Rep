FITNESS

Rejuvenate and recharge at our state-of-the-art Fitness Centre. The 325-square-metre (3,500-square-foot) fitness area offers a leading-edge gym fully outfitted with free weights and strength-training and cardio equipment by Matrix and a yoga studio. Located on the eighth floor, the Fitness Centre is accessible 24 hours a day, with a room or residence key, for registered Hotel guests and residents only. Minimum age for use is 12 years.
